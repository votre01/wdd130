BOOK A TRIP

Since 1990, we've helped more than 200,000 people of all ages enjoy the best outdoor experience of their lives. Whether it's for one day or a two-week adventure vacation close to home or a foreign land, our experienced, professional, and customer-focused guides make all the difference. We offer a variety of trips to take you on an unforgettable adventure.

Rough tides weekends

Our most popular trip loved by all. Bring your family, friends and yes, rivals to this guided awesome two day trip adventure! You will enjoy various adventures on our different best spots on the river.

Rafty summer tournament

Only the best time to get the rafting games afloat. This is our greatest tournament that occurs every summer. Form a team and apply for our Rafty Summer tournament. Great prizes are available to be scooped up.

The scholar's adventure

All scholars need to step out of the classroom some time. This 10 day camp adventure is great for all scholars. From primary school to college scholars, we will provide the best experience to refresh your mind.

Work hard, play hard

Yes, teams need to take a break from work and to regroup. Three days of non-stop fun will do the trick. Great adventures and activities that will get teams to go back to work with energy and motivation.

For the strength of youth

Who doesn't want to be great at rafting, right. For the strength of youth, is a two week training for young players, ages 12-18. Professional instructors will provide both theory and practical training.

Happy couples float

We want a happy world and we believe love is the key. We can take care of your couples retreat with our Happy couples float trip. An awesome experince for couples for 5 days that will create an unbreakable bond.
